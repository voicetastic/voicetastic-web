Voice + text over Meshtastic, in your browser

Plug in a Meshtastic radio over USB and Connect. Text and voice both run voicetastic-core's protocol core in the browser — Codec2 (pure Rust) for voice, decoded and played back here. Chrome/Edge or Firefox 151+ over localhost/HTTPS; voice also needs mic permission.

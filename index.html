Connect your radio

Plug in a Meshtastic radio over USB. Text and voice both run voicetastic-core's protocol core in the browser — Codec2 (pure Rust) for voice, RNNoise for capture-side denoise. Chrome/Edge or Firefox 151+ over localhost or HTTPS; voice also needs mic permission.

Click Connect, then pick the serial port.

Settings

Device configuration — Owner, LoRa, Device, Position, Power, Network, Display, Bluetooth, plus per-channel settings. Each card mirrors what the desktop GUI's Settings tab shows: the form is populated from the live ProtocolState snapshot, and Apply rebuilds a config message via core's admin_packet and sends it to your radio.
